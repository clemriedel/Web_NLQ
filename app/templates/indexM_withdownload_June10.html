Natural Language Quantification

Natural Language Quantification permits to: 1/ Find: patterns, topics, concepts in documents 2/ Quantify: money, votes, views, likes generated by each topic 3/ Assist you in the creation of high-impact documents. NLQ can be applied to any activity described by words and quantified by numbers. You can learn how to create your own implimentation of NLQ at clemriedel/edu (github).

NLQ is the fruit of a dynamic and flexible collaboration between indiviudals that sometimes do not even know each others. Thanks.
